Explore Our Facilities

At iGrow Montessori, we believe that the early years are the foundation of a child's lifelong journey. Our preschool and daycare offer a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where curiosity is nurtured, and learning begins through play, exploration, and gentle guidance. From Pre-Nursery to UKG, every child is encouraged to grow at their own pace — with care, creativity, and confidence.

Our Programs

Play School

Age Group: 2.5 to 5 years

Our Play School program is thoughtfully designed to provide a warm, joyful, and engaging learning space where little ones take their first steps into structured education. The curriculum blends storytelling, music, dance, games, and crafts to spark curiosity and encourage self-expression.

Key Features:

  • Theme-Based Learning: Each month focuses on a new theme like animals, seasons, or festivals to make learning exciting and relatable.
  • Activity Corners: Includes reading nooks, art zones, and sensory play areas for hands-on exploration.
  • Emotional Development: Teachers focus on kindness, sharing, and social behavior to help children build confidence and empathy.
  • Language and Motor Skills: Songs, rhymes, and drawing activities nurture communication and fine motor abilities.
  • Celebrations & Events: Children participate in national festivals, birthday celebrations, and cultural days for joyful memories and learning.
"Our goal is to ensure each child leaves school with a smile, a story, and a new skill every day."

Daycare

Age Group: 2 to 6 years

Our Daycare facility is a second home for children—safe, affectionate, and enriching. Whether parents need short-term care or full-day support, we offer flexible and structured routines that suit every family's needs.

Key Features:

  • Age-Wise Groups: Infants, toddlers, and school-goers are cared for in separate age-appropriate zones.
  • Daily Routine: A balance of rest, meals, play, and learning. Includes storytelling sessions, indoor games, and quiet time.
  • Nutritious Meals: Healthy, home-style food prepared fresh each day. We also accommodate special dietary requirements if needed.
  • Health & Hygiene: Clean sleeping mats, regular sanitization, and supervised toileting practices ensure cleanliness.
  • Safety & Supervision: CCTV monitoring, trained staff, emergency protocols, and regular check-ins with parents.
  • Homework Support (for older kids): Dedicated time and assistance with school assignments after school hours.
"We know how precious your child is—and we promise to care for them just like you would."

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours of the play school and daycare?

Our play school runs from 9:00 AM to 1:30 PM, while daycare services are available from 8:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.

Is the facility air-conditioned and child-safe?

Yes, classrooms and play areas are fully air-conditioned, equipped with cushioned flooring, rounded furniture, and child-safe utilities.

Do you provide transportation services?

Yes, we offer safe and supervised pick-up and drop-off services within selected localities. Each vehicle has a lady attendant onboard.

How are meals and snacks managed?

Nutritious vegetarian meals and snacks are prepared in-house under hygienic conditions. Weekly menus are shared with parents in advance.

Are there CCTV cameras installed?

Yes, the entire premises is under 24/7 CCTV surveillance, and live access may be provided to parents during daycare hours.

What qualifications do your teachers and staff have?

All staff members are trained in early childhood education and undergo regular first-aid and safety workshops.

How do you handle emergencies or illnesses?

We have a tie-up with a nearby pediatric clinic and always have a first-aid kit and emergency contacts on file. Parents are informed immediately.

Can parents visit during school hours?

Parents are welcome for scheduled visits and events. Unscheduled visits are regulated to maintain a secure and distraction-free environment.

Do you organize field trips or outdoor activities?

Yes, we conduct supervised picnics, nature walks, and local visits to parks or cultural sites, depending on the age group.

Is there a settling-in period for new children?

Absolutely. We offer a flexible settling-in period where parents can stay for short durations to help the child adapt comfortably.
